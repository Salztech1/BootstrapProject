Full Stack Web Development

This practical hands-on front-end web design and web
development training in Cameroon gives you full training on front

In this front-end web design and web development training, you
will learn basic to advanced skills that every web developer needs
to know.

Develop the skills you need in order to be able to make websites
from scratch. This is the beginning of your website design and

Learn to use HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React , Mongo DB, and
Express together in building modern beautiful, fully functional
amazing websites.

Target:

  • every young passionate girl in university or higher education already studying or has basic knowledge or ideas in any tech-related area.
  • Every young girl passionate about tech with no experience in tech
  • Working Professionals switching careers

Targeted Number: 25 Students, 20 girls, 5 boys

Targeted Students: We’ll target students in their 2nd, 3rd & final year in the universities around Buea. All students coming from out of Buea must have where to live during the course duration.

Duration

3 months course on Full-stack Web Development

Tentative Start Date: November 8th, 2022

Registration and Selection Process:

Students will apply through an online form, and pre-selection will be done immediately
Interviews will be done in the following week of application. Pre-selected students will be contacted
The final selection will be done immediately after the interview. The selected students will be informed.

An Orientation date will be decided in the second week of November, this will also double as the start/opening date. Classes will begin immediately. Attendance during orientation is mandatory.

NB: The nature of the interview will be decided upon by the interview team.

Selection Must-Haves (Requirement policies)
  • Must possess a laptop
  • Must be based in Buea
  • Must sign the contract
  • Must be willing to learn.
  • Must attend all classes. 3 Consecutive absences and you are kicked out but will still pay fees.
  • Must agree to all terms and conditions.
Fee
